Italy

Italy is a state in Southern Europe located on the Apennine Peninsula. The capital is Rome. Known for its ancient history, art, architecture, fashion and cuisine (pasta, pizza). The official language is Italian. Italy is the birthplace of the Roman Empire, the Vatican and the cradle of the Renaissance. It is a member of the EU, NATO and the UN.
